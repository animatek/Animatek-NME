Animatek NME - Nord Modular Editor G1 (Windows)
===============================================

64-bit build for Windows 10/11.

FIRST LAUNCH
------------
The binary is not code-signed, so Windows SmartScreen may show
"Windows protected your PC" on first run. Click "More info" and then
"Run anyway". You only need to do this once.

CONNECTING YOUR NORD MODULAR
----------------------------
Connect a MIDI interface to the synth's PC IN/OUT ports, open the app and
pick the MIDI ports under Device > MIDI Settings.
