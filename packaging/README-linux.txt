Animatek NME - Nord Modular Editor G1 (Linux)
=============================================

64-bit build, made on Ubuntu 22.04 - it runs on most distros from 2022
onwards. Requires ALSA and X11 (standard on desktop installs).

RUNNING
-------
If the file lost its executable bit when unzipping:

    chmod +x AnimatekNME
    ./AnimatekNME

CONNECTING YOUR NORD MODULAR
----------------------------
Connect a MIDI interface to the synth's PC IN/OUT ports, open the app and
pick the MIDI ports under Device > MIDI Settings.
